Indoor/Outdoor

Transform your outdoor space into a comfortable place to relax, eat, and gather. There’s no reason your backyard deck or rooftop hangout has to be cold and sparse. Use outdoor cushions, lounge chair pillows, and boat cushion upholstery from Beckenstein Fabrics & Interiors to make it warm, inviting, and interesting. Outdoor and indoor fabrics do double duty. They work as well on the patio as they do in the living room. What makes them unique is their resistance to both fading and staining. They also stand tough against mildew brought on by wet conditions. Able to withstand both sun and rain, heat and damp, outdoor cushions and upholstery are perfectly suited for any patio, deck, roof, balcony, or backyard. Give us a call or visit our New York City store, located in the heart of Manhattan, to choose from a wide selection of beautiful and durable fabrics with which to accent your outdoor furniture.

Wallpaper

Beckenstein Fabric and Interiors has a huge selection of wallpaper in NYC. There are thousands of patterns, colors and textures that wallpaper can offer. Polka dots, plaid, floral, geometric shapes and many other patterns to meet your decorating needs and personal style. Textured wall coverings can add depth and dimension to your walls which paint cannot. Some new wallpaper even has beading, sequins, lace, and ribbons that add more drama and style to a bare wall. You can even coordinate your wallpaper with your fabrics, so everything in your home is cohesive. It’s a wonderful way to bring elegant style to any room in your home. Our Exclusive Wallpaper Selections vary from Romo, to Scalamandre, to Thibaut, just to name a few. Visit our showroom where one of our designers can guide you through the selection process.
