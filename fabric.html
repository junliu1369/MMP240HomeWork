Fabrics

Beckenstein Fabrics & Interiors does top-of-the-line work. We have thousands of designer fabrics as well as exclusive fabric patterns from the finest decorative mills around the world—cottons, linens, silks, and blended fibers specifically designed and used for home furnishings. We represent the top decorative fabric lines, showing hundreds of possibilities for home fabrics. Interior Design Services are available too!

Come visit our showroom for a fabric and design consultation. See our beautiful fabrics or order fabric samples and swatches from our fabric library to view in your home. We offer over 2,000 sample books from all of the major fabric vendors and mills. With exclusive fabric selections from Kravet and others, our design staff is familiar with our selection and can help you find what you are looking for.
